The Unforgetable Pabellon

Pabellon Criollo is a traditional Venezuelan dish consisting of shredded beef, black beans, white rice, and fried plantains.

  • 1 lb shredded beef
  • 1 can black beans
  • 2 cups white rice
  • 2 ripe plantains
  • Cook the shredded beef
  • Prepare black beans
  • Cook white rice
  • Fry the ripe plantains
  • Enjoy it
