The Famous Arepas

Arepas are a staple in Venezuelan cuisine. They are round, flatbreads made of ground maize dough or cooked flour, usually filled with cheese, ham, or other ingredients.

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Mix everything in a bowl
  • Cook in a pan
  • Fillings of your choice
  • Enjoy it
